Youssef Nehme Tohme is the Chairman of the Board of Managers of Almabani General Contractors LLC. In his capacity, he holds directorship and executive position in the companies’ activities and business. Born in 1974, Youssef has received both his Bachelors’ and Masters’ degree in Civil Engineering from City, University of London and is a Member of the Institute of Civil Engineers. Youssef started his professional career working in the Oil and Gas sector where he built the business bottom-up tuning Levant Oil LLC. company into an international trading, importation, storage and distribution company with an annual turnover of 720,000 metric tons yearly of refined gasoline and gasoil products thru a terminal storage facility of 55,000 metric tons. In his capacity as CEO, his activities covered the Levant and West African region turning the company into an international multi-million Dollar player. Youssef joined Almabani General Contractors in 2009 as Vice President and through his managerial experience and civil engineering background helped manage all activities across Almabanis’ business. Youssef served up until 2020 as Member of Board and Member of the Steering Committee of the Riyadh Metro Project overlooking the execution of the multi-billion Saudi Riyals mega project. In 2020 Youssef was promoted to Chairman of the Board of Almabani General Contractors, where he serves as a board member as well as overlooks the daily activities of the company.
