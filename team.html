Youssef N. Tohme

Chairman...

Youssef Nehme Tohme is the Chairman of the Board of Managers of Almabani General Contractors LLC. In his capacity, he holds directorship and executive position in the companies’ activities and business. Born in 1974, Youssef has received both his Bachelors’ and Masters’ degree in Civil Engineering from City, University of London and is a Member of the Institute of Civil Engineers. Youssef started his professional career working in the Oil and Gas sector where he built the business bottom-up tuning Levant Oil LLC. company into an international trading, importation, storage and distribution company with an annual turnover of 720,000 metric tons yearly of refined gasoline and gasoil products thru a terminal storage facility of 55,000 metric tons. In his capacity as CEO, his activities covered the Levant and West African region turning the company into an international multi-million Dollar player. Youssef joined Almabani General Contractors in 2009 as Vice President and through his managerial experience and civil engineering background helped manage all activities across Almabanis’ business. Youssef served up until 2020 as Member of Board and Member of the Steering Committee of the Riyadh Metro Project overlooking the execution of the multi-billion Saudi Riyals mega project. In 2020 Youssef was promoted to Chairman of the Board of Almabani General Contractors, where he serves as a board member as well as overlooks the daily activities of the company. Youssef heads the Nehme & Therese Foundation a private charity, non-profit organization that helps the underprivileged come out of extreme poverty. The foundation has helped in the last 15 years in the circa of 100,000 families giving them access to education, work opportunities, agricultural enablers and healthcare to help them lead honorable lives. Youssef is an active investor in innovative technologies and renewable energy companies, he serves as a board member in a variety of businesses in the region.

Management

Almabani management team is behind the company’s success and sustained growth and expansion. Dedication, expertise, professionalism and commitment are key elements of the management policy.

The management team key performance is based on consistency, clarity and steadiness of purpose, and thorough leadership; these same principles are inspired by the new generation of employees joining the company.

Almabani continues to invest in quality, value engineering, proactive and talented people, endeavoring at all times to satisfy our client's expectations and to best serve the welfare of our employees and society.

In the years to come, we will continue to carry the flag fueled by an inherent culture of steady growth, quality work and commitment to timely achievements.

Finance & Accounting

Finance and Accounting are service departments. They serve as the financial background for all the activities of the company where all the records are kept and managed. Their main tasks are:

  • To respond to the internal top management’s business needs.
  • To provide accurate financial information to all other departments.
  • To liaise with the financial institutions that constitute the backbone of the company’s activities.
  • To develop for the company’s shareholders financial reports to review the activities and performance of the company.
  • To meet all legal requirements inherent to us.
  • To ensure that books and records are kept up-to-date according to defined policies and procedures set by the management.
