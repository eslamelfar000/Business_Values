Aviation

Aviation Almabani takes particular pride in its contributions to the Kingdom's air, sea, and land based defense projects. One of the foremost players in this vital area, the company can surely be said to be a leader in this field. Almabani's projects portfolio in this segment includes runways, aprons, control towers, terminal buildings, and related facilities. Independently, and in collaboration with international companies, Almabani has erected several aircraft shelters featuring state-of-the-art technologies. Nuclear, chemical and bacteriological personnel shelters fitted out with high-tech equipment are also on the company's projects list.

  • Almabani has also been involved in turnkey projects all over the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It has worked on a wide range of infrastructural projects for the Ministry of Defense & Aviation (MODA). The third runway at Jeddah's King Abdulaziz International Airport was built in a record time of six months - employing a workforce of eight hundred people working round the clock, without interruption to the airport's operations - a task that required a logistical operation of high precision.

  • Almabani has also completed a series of projects in the 'Empty Quarter'- an isolated area in the desert, notorious for its inhospitable terrain. These projects include 5 airstrips and supporting facilities for the Frontiers Forces of the Ministry of Interior and a full size runway at Kharkheer for the Ministry of Defense & Aviation. These demanding projects called for a high degree of logistical coordination and resource management. .

  • Besides new airports, Almabani has been involved in several airport upgrades and refurbishment projects. They range from adding royal pavilions, VIP lounge, aircraft repair hangers, airfield service buildings and cargo terminals to air terminal refurbishment and wide-bodied jet runway upgrades.
